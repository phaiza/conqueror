Fingerprint

Split second security

Our fingerprint sensor unlocks your Conqueror i7 in just 0.2 seconds, even when the screen is turned off. Crafted from premium ceramic, the fingerprint sensor actively learns and progressively improves, so your scans get even more precise over time. It’s fast, it’s accurate, and it’s secure.

Display

Go beyond the scope.

Correct decisions require perfect vision. Accurate colour reproduction and HD-resolution screen (720 x 1280) provide a perfect image. The narrow scope increases the width of the display, and its handy size allows you to control your smartphone with one hand. The smartphone will always look new thanks to the scratch-resistant Asahi glass.

Cameras

Snap to impress

Lightning-fast shutter speed, snappy autofocus, and intelligent software combine to create unmatched smartphone camera experience. Every shot taken with our 16 MP rear camera is sharp and crisp, thanks to cutting-edge noise reduction and improved stabilization technologies. A new 16 MP front camera allows you to capture every moment at every angle.

Design

See More, Hold Less.

Unlike other phones with a similar screen size, the large 5.7” display fits in your hand, giving you a big screen experience without the inconvenience of a big phone. The LG G6 is sculpted from aluminum and glass and is available in platinum and black. The 18:9 FullVision display with rounded corners and narrow bezels conclude the harmonious design.

  • Resolution18:9
  • Format 80%
  • Body to screen ratio 5.7"

Technology

Don't worry, we got this.

We packed 6GB of LPDDR4 RAM into the Conqueror i7. Overkill? Maybe. Awesome? Yes. The top-of-the-line Qualcomm® Snapdragon™ 820 processor and Adreno™ 530 GPU make apps, games, and other intensive applications a breeze. Dual nano SIM support means you’ll always be connected, and with our vivid 5.5” Optic AMOLED display, all of that power under the hood is just as nice to look at as it is to use.
